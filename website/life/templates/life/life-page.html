About



I've always considered myself as a "cool" nerd. I love getting out, being active, and meeting new people, but when the times right, I love showing off my inner geekiness. Some of my favorite hobbies include, playing various sports, reading anything and everything, playing both board games and video games, and buying clothes; sometimes even watching videos of others buying clothes. If any of this sounds like something interesting to you, than, well, we might just have a thing or two in common. {%comment%}I'm always down to play some volleyball, some soccer, or even some table tennis when taking out the old paddle is warranted. You'll often find me reading anything from fantasy to philosophical and maybe even a biography here and there. {%endcomment%}
